About Our Project

Our project involves developing a system to assist in rehabilitation and physiotherapy processes for patients. The idea stemmed from the need for a system that would reduce the burden on the healthcare system and expedite the rehabilitation process for patients by increasing availability of physiotherapy appointment slots.

The system includes:

  • A walking track with adjustable obstacle heights
  • Sensors to detect contact and collisions with obstacles
  • User interface for physiotherapists to input customized training programs
  • User interface for patients to perform exercises and track progress
  • Real-time data collection for performance analysis

The system is designed to enable patients to perform walking and balance exercises independently while being remotely monitored by a physiotherapist.

Key benefits:

  • Saves healthcare system time and resources
  • Shortens patient waiting times and rehabilitation process
  • Ability to treat larger number of patients
  • Continuous progress and performance tracking
  • Customized training programs for each patient

The system is currently in prototype development and testing stages. We aim to expand its use among rehabilitation centers and hospitals nationwide.
